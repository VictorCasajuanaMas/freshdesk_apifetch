Short Description:

App Description:
You can see more information If you have your own API that contains additional information from your freshdesk contacts such as contracted products, debt status, etc... through this App you can connect to show it when you are modifying a ticket or a contact from freshdesk

Instructions:
Once installed, the application is automatic, you don't have to take any action, when you enter a ticket or a contact, it already connects with your API and shows the result.
